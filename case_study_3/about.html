Our Story

🏫

Adventist Academy Bacolod is a Seventh-day Adventist institution that has been proudly serving the Bacolod community for decades. Founded on the principles of holistic, Christ-centered education, we have grown into one of the most trusted schools in Western Visayas.

Our school offers a comprehensive curriculum that develops the mental, physical, social, and spiritual dimensions of every student — ensuring that graduates are well-rounded, morally grounded, and ready for the challenges of the modern world.

We are proud of our diverse, inclusive, and faith-filled community, and we welcome every family who shares our vision of education that truly matters.
