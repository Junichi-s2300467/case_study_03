Mission & Vision

The principles and purpose that guide everything we do at Adventist Academy Bacolod.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide a Christ-centered, holistic education that develops the mental, physical, social, and spiritual dimensions of every student — equipping them to serve God and humanity with excellence and integrity.

🌟 Our Vision

To be a leading Seventh-day Adventist institution in Western Visayas that produces graduates of strong faith, academic excellence, and servant leadership — ready to make a positive difference in their communities and the world.

Our Core Values

These values are the foundation of our school culture and guide our decisions every day.

  • ✝️ Faith in God
  • 🤝 Integrity & Honesty
  • 💡 Academic Excellence
  • ❤️ Compassion & Respect
  • 🙏 Service & Humility
  • 🏆 Excellence in All Things

Our Commitment

We commit to every family that their child will be seen, heard, and valued. Our teachers receive ongoing professional development, our facilities are continually upgraded, and our programs are regularly reviewed to reflect the best in modern pedagogy and the evolving needs of society.

At Adventist Academy Bacolod, education is not a transaction — it is a lifelong relationship built on trust, respect, and shared aspiration.
